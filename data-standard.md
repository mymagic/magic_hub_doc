### Data Type

#### Startup Organisation

* Organisation Name 
* Legal Form \(e.g. Sdn Bhd, LLP, etc\)
* Organisation Registration Number \(e.g. SSM\)
* Address 
* Country 
* State 
* City 
* Contact Email 
* Industry 
* Year Founded 
* Website

#### Social Enterprise Organisation

* Organisation Name 
* Legal Form \(e.g. Sdn Bhd, LLP, etc\)
* Organisation Registration Number \(e.g. SSM\)
* Address 
* Country 
* State 
* City 
* Contact Email 
* Primary SDG 
* Industry 
* Year Founded 
* Website

#### Individual

* Email \(verified\)
* Full name 
* IC Number / Passport 
* Country 
* State 
* Race 
* Gender 
* Date of Birth 
* Mobile Number 
* Persona \(Student, Aspiring entrepreneurs, Startup founder, etc\)
